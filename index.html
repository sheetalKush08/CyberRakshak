Cyber Crime Awareness

Stay Alert. Stay Secure. Learn about common cyber threats and how to protect yourself online.

🎣

Phishing Attacks

Fraudulent emails, messages, or websites designed to steal sensitive information like passwords and banking details.

  • Never click unknown links
  • Verify sender emails carefully
  • Enable two-factor authentication
📱

OTP & UPI Fraud

Cyber criminals trick users into sharing OTPs or approving fake UPI payment requests.

  • Never share OTP with anyone
  • Verify payment requests
  • Use trusted payment apps only
🔐

Password Security

Weak passwords can expose your accounts to hacking and identity theft.

  • Use strong passwords
  • Mix symbols and numbers
  • Change passwords regularly
👤

Identity Theft

Personal data stolen online can be used for fraud, fake accounts, and scams.

  • Protect Aadhaar & PAN details
  • Avoid public WiFi for banking
  • Monitor suspicious activities
💻

Social Media Scams

Fake profiles and malicious links on social media can compromise your accounts.

  • Do not trust unknown profiles
  • Enable account privacy
  • Report suspicious activity
🛡️

Safe Browsing

Unsafe websites and downloads can infect your device with malware and spyware.

  • Visit secure HTTPS websites
  • Install antivirus software
  • Avoid pirated downloads
